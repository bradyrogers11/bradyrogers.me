9/23/2024 – Fiber Technician with Metronet

As a Fiber Technician at Metronet, a leading internet service provider, I oversee the installation and maintenance of fiber optic internet from the street's terminal directly to both residential and commercial properties. My daily responsibilities include managing all aspects of fiber runs, ensuring the integrity and efficiency of fiber optic cables, and utilizing advanced tools such as Optical Time-Domain Reflectometers (OTDRs) and fiber splicers to perform high-quality installations and repairs. I adhere strictly to safety protocols, particularly while operating the company truck. I install and configure ONTs, routers and use my laptop along with other testing tools to conduct quality of service tests—ensuring customer satisfaction and network reliability. I also diagnose and resolve network connectivity issues, including troubleshooting DNS and DHCP errors to maintain seamless internet performance.
